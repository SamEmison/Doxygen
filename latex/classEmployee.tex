\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an employee in the company. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classEmployee}{Employee}} class manages information about an employee, including their unique ID, years of service, hourly pay rate, and hours worked. It provides methods to calculate pay, handle anniversaries, and print the employee\textquotesingle{}s details.
